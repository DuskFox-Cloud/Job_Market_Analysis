# 📊 Job Market Analysis Report | EDA Project

> A complete **Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) project** on **742 job postings** to uncover salary trends, hiring hotspots, role demand, and skill requirements in the data job market.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview
This project analyzes a dataset of **742 job postings** across multiple industries to identify:

- 📍 **Top hiring states**
- 💰 **Salary trends by location and role**
- 🏢 **Industry and company hiring patterns**
- 🧠 **Role-based skill requirements**
- 🎓 **Impact of education on salary**

The goal is to transform raw job market data into **actionable career and hiring insights**.

---

## 📌 Key Insights
- 🌟 **California (CA)** leads in both **job volume** and **average salary**
- 🐍 **Python and SQL** are the most demanded technical skills
- 🧬 **Biotech & Pharmaceuticals** is the top hiring industry
- 💼 **Data Scientist** is the most in-demand job title
- ☁️ **AWS + Spark** dominate Data Engineering roles
- 🎓 **Ph.D. candidates earn the highest average salaries**
